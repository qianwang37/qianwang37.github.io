Research

My research lies at the intersection of entrepreneurship, strategy, and venture financing, with a focus on founder demographics. I study how entrepreneurs overcome early-stage constraints, focusing on the roles of human capital and institutional change. I also examine how firms navigate uncertainty in their markets, particularly how they respond to changes in their competitive and policy environments.
