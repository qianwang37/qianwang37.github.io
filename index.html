Hello! I am Qian (pronounced Chi-an), a Ph.D. candidate in Management at the Johnson Graduate School of Management, Cornell University. I study entrepreneurship, strategy, and venture financing. My research examines how entrepreneurs overcome early-stage constraints, focusing on the roles of human capital and institutional change.

My work has been published in Management Science and Organization Science, and has been recognized with the 2026 Bradford-Osborne Research Award (UW Foster & UCLA) and the 2025 DRUID Steven Klepper Award for Best Young Scholar Paper.

I am on the 2026–2027 academic job market.
