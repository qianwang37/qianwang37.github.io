A Few Things About Me

  • Hometown: I was born and raised in Weifang, Shandong Province, in eastern China. It's considered a smaller city by Chinese standards, even though more than 9 million people live there.
  • First-generation student: I'm a first-generation college student. My parents began working at eighteen and have worked hard ever since. The opportunity to pursue a Ph.D. is a privilege I don't take for granted.
  • Family: I met my wife, Bingyue, at the airport on our way to Ithaca. She's an attorney, though I hope you'll never need her help.
  • Sports: I've been a Los Angeles Lakers fan since the 2007–2008 season, although these days I play more badminton than basketball.
  • Lucky numbers: My lucky numbers are 3 and 7, so you may notice "37" in many of my usernames and accounts.
  • People: I'm pretty bad at recognizing faces, so please don't take it personally if I need a gentle reminder. But I do tend to remember people's stories, backgrounds, and life experiences.
  • Colors: I'm also a bit color-blind, so if my outfit looks more creative than intended, please feel free to let me know.
