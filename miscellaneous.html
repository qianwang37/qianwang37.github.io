A Few Things About Me

  • I was born and raised in Weifang, Shandong Province, in eastern China. It is often described as a smaller city by Chinese standards, despite having a population of more than 9 million.
  • I am a first-generation college student. My parents started working when they were eighteen and have worked hard ever since. Having the opportunity to pursue a Ph.D. is a privilege I do not take for granted.
  • I met my wife, Bingyue, at an airport on our way to Ithaca. She's an attorney, though I hope you'll never need her help.
  • I have been a big Los Angeles Lakers fan since the 2007–2008 season, although these days I play more badminton than basketball.
  • My lucky numbers are 3 and 7, so you may notice "37" appearing in many of my usernames and accounts.
  • I am not great at recognizing faces, so please do not take it personally if I need a gentle reminder. That said, I tend to remember people's stories, backgrounds, and life experiences super well.
  • I am also a bit color-blind, so if my outfit looks more creative than intended, please feel free to let me know.
